About Volt Audio

Volt Audio is a powerful text-to-speech tool that allows you to convert text into audio and download it in various formats.

Help Topics

How do I convert text to speech?

Simply type your text in the box, click "Convert", and download your audio.

Which audio formats are supported?

Volt Audio supports MP3, WAV, and OGG formats.

How can I install Volt Audio as an app?

Click the install prompt when it appears, or go to your browser menu and select "Install".
